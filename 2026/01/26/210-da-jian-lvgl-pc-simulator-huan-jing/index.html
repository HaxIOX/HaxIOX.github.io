步骤

1.下载lvgl

2.下载lv_port_pc_vscode

2.安装mingw-w64并添加环境变量，这个我倒是没添加，但不知道为啥会好使

3.Download CMake

4.下载SDL

5.将lvgl下所有内容复制到lv_port_pc_vscode的lvgl文件夹下，将SDL2-2.32.10复制到lv_port_pc_vscode

6.打开vscode，顶部命令栏选择，显示并运行命令，然后点击cmake：选择工具包，然后点击gcc

7.左下角或者右下角点击编译

8.报错是正常的，添加sdl文件地址

# Find and include SDL2 library
set(CMAKE_PREFIX_PATH "${PROJECT_SOURCE_DIR}/SDL2-2.32.10/x86_64-w64-mingw32" ${CMAKE_PREFIX_PATH})
file(COPY "${PROJECT_SOURCE_DIR}/SDL2-2.32.10/x86_64-w64-mingw32/bin/SDL2.dll" DESTINATION ${PROJECT_SOURCE_DIR}/bin)

8.在main文件中注释*// #include "glob.h"并添加#include LV_SDL_INCLUDE_PATH
